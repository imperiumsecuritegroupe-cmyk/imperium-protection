Residence Security

Comprehensive protection of your home, property or business premises: security audit, equipment installation and dedicated physical presence — 24 hours a day.

Request a Security Audit

Overview

Your home as a protected sanctuary

The residence is the primary space of life and the most valued asset to protect. Yet it is also a predictable location, making it a preferred target for various threats: burglary, intrusion, surveillance or direct attack.

Our residence security service combines a technical approach (vulnerability audit, security equipment installation) with a human approach (physical presence, static agents). This dual response guarantees total protection of your living space.

Every intervention starts with an exhaustive audit of the property: identification of weak points, assessment of existing systems and personalised recommendations ranked by priority.

What is included

Our residence security services

Vulnerability audit

Complete assessment of your property's security vulnerabilities: access points, CCTV coverage gaps, perimeter weaknesses and identified risks.

Technical equipment installation

Installation of advanced CCTV systems, access control, alarms, anti-intrusion devices and secure communications — all discreetly integrated into your environment.

Static agent presence

Deployment of one or more agents at your property: gate security, perimeter surveillance, access control and immediate threat response.

Remote surveillance

24/7 monitoring of your property from our operations centre, with immediate response capability and real-time alerting.

Integrated 24/7 protocols

Permanent round-the-clock protection with rotation of security teams, rapid response procedures and direct coordination with emergency services.

Frequently asked questions

Your questions, our answers

Can you secure a property I don't currently occupy?

Yes. We provide security for unoccupied properties as well as occupied ones. Remote surveillance, periodic patrols and physical presence are all available.

Will the security equipment be visible?

We work discreetly. Equipment is integrated to be as unobtrusive as possible while remaining fully effective. A deterrent or discreet approach is possible depending on your preference.

Can you secure a rented property or hotel suite?

Yes. We can secure any temporary accommodation: hotel suites, rented villas or any other type of property, regardless of duration.

What happens if an intrusion attempt is detected?

Our response protocols are activated immediately: on-site agent intervention, crisis unit alert and if necessary, coordination with law enforcement. Response time under 2 minutes on the perimeter.
