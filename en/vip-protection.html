Overview

Protection designed for public exposure

Public figures face unique security challenges. Their visibility, media exposure and public interactions create specific vulnerabilities that standard protection cannot address. Our VIP service is specifically designed to account for these particularities.

We develop discreet security arrangements that allow you to maintain your public presence without restriction, while benefiting from total protection. Each protocol is tailored to your schedule, habits and specific exposure level.

Our specialists also work on managing your digital footprint, protecting your private information and preventing intrusions into your personal sphere.

Frequently asked questions

Your questions, our answers

Can protection remain invisible to the public?

Yes. Our agents are experts at blending into any environment. They can appear as personal assistants, event staff or ordinary individuals while maintaining full protection.

How do you handle press and paparazzi situations?

We have specific protocols for managing media pressure: securing exit routes, working with event press officers and if necessary, legally blocking intrusive photography.

Can you coordinate with my existing team (assistant, manager)?

Absolutely. Our team integrates seamlessly with your existing staff. We establish clear communication channels and coordinate all logistics with your personal team.

What happens in the event of a threat or incident?

We have immediate response protocols: controlled evacuation, crisis unit activation and if necessary, coordination with law enforcement. Every scenario is anticipated in advance.
