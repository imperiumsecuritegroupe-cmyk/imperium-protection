Intelligence & Risk Analysis

Human and technical intelligence to anticipate threats before they materialise. Proactive monitoring, risk profiling and personalised strategic recommendations for those who choose prevention over reaction.

Request an Intelligence Briefing

Overview

Anticipate rather than react

Intelligence is the foundation of effective protection. Knowing a threat before it manifests allows it to be neutralised without confrontation, protecting both the individual and those around them while maintaining the discretion that elite protection demands.

Our intelligence and risk analysis service combines human sources (HUMINT), technical analysis (OSINT, digital monitoring) and behavioural assessment to provide you with an accurate, up-to-date picture of your threat environment.

Whether it is a specific threat, a concern about a particular individual, the preparation of a sensitive journey or ongoing monitoring of your risk exposure, we have the expertise to give you the intelligence advantage.

What is included

Our intelligence & risk analysis services

Personal risk assessment

In-depth analysis of your exposure profile: public presence, professional activities, known adversaries and assessment of your current threat level.

Open source intelligence (OSINT)

Systematic monitoring of open sources — social media, press, forums, dark web — to detect early warning signals of threats directed at you.

Behavioural profiling

Assessment of potentially threatening individuals: behavioural analysis, threat credibility evaluation and recommended management strategies.

Destination intelligence

Pre-travel intelligence reports for sensitive destinations: geopolitical analysis, crime statistics, risk zones, key contacts and contingency plans.

Proactive continuous monitoring

24/7 monitoring service with real-time alerts on any new threats detected, weekly reports and direct access to our analysts for urgent situations.

Frequently asked questions

Your questions, our answers

Are your intelligence methods legal?

Absolutely. All our intelligence collection methods are strictly legal and comply with French and European law. We never conduct illegal interceptions or intrusive surveillance without legal authorisation.

How long does an intelligence assessment take?

Delivery times vary depending on the scope of the mission: from 48 hours for a targeted threat assessment to several weeks for comprehensive continuous monitoring.

Will my personal information remain confidential?

All information you share with us is subject to strict confidentiality and is used exclusively for the conduct of your mission. A non-disclosure agreement is signed at the start of each engagement.

Can you monitor an individual who concerns me?

Yes, within a strictly legal framework. We can carry out a behavioural and threat assessment of an individual who concerns you, using legal open source and investigative methods.
