Overview

Security without borders

International travel — whether for business or personal reasons — exposes individuals to specific risks that vary greatly depending on the destination: political instability, targeted crime, kidnapping, surveillance or terrorism. Our international escort service provides end-to-end secure coverage, from departure to return.

We rely on a certified partner network present in more than 40 countries to ensure local continuity of protection while maintaining Imperium's operational standards and protocols.

Each international mission begins with an in-depth analysis of the destination country: political context, crime statistics, local threat assessment and contingency planning for all identified scenarios.

Frequently asked questions

Your questions, our answers

Do you operate in conflict zones?

Yes. We have specialised teams trained for hostile environments. However, each mission is subject to a rigorous feasibility assessment to ensure the safety of our teams and our client.

How quickly can you deploy internationally?

Depending on the destination and preparation level, we can deploy within 24 to 72 hours. For planned missions, we recommend a minimum of one week's notice for optimal preparation.

Do your local partners meet the same standards as your agents?

Yes. All our international partners are carefully vetted, certified according to local standards and trained in our operating protocols. We do not work with operators who do not meet our security and ethics standards.

Can you organise protection for an entire family?

Absolutely. We arrange multi-person protection covering all family members, with coordination between several teams if necessary.
