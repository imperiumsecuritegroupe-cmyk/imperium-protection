Local presence

The highest standard for the principality

Monaco is the principality of the ultra-wealthy, royal families and the world's great fortunes. Its exceptional concentration of wealth and high-value personalities makes it one of the most demanding environments for private security. Absolute discretion is non-negotiable.

Imperium Private Protection operates regularly in Monaco, in coordination with Principality authorities. We have mastered local specificities: access to the Rock, Palace protocols, Monaco Grand Prix events, the Red Cross Gala and security management of superyachts in Port Hercule.

Our Monegasque clientele includes princely families, billionaire residents and high-value visitors. Every mission is handled with an absolute level of discretion and professionalism, consistent with the Principality's exacting standards.
