Local presence

The capital of the French Riviera, secured

Nice is the natural hub of the French Riviera, welcoming year-round an international wealthy clientele of business personalities, cultural figures and sports stars. The city concentrates luxury hotels, Michelin-starred restaurants and prestigious events that attract high-value, high-exposure profiles.

Imperium Private Protection maintains permanent teams deployed across Nice and the entire Alpes-Maritimes department. Our agents are operational from the Promenade des Anglais to the Carré d'Or, from the residential hills of Cimiez to the private marinas of the port.

Our knowledge of the Nice terrain, its neighbourhoods, tourist flows and events (Carnival, MIPIM, nearby Monaco Grand Prix) allows us to anticipate and manage every situation with precision.

Local analysis

Security environment on the French Riviera

Despite its image, the French Riviera has specific risks: armed robberies at luxury hotels, jewellery heists, organised pickpockets targeting wealthy tourists, and risks linked to media exposure of high-profile personalities. Our permanent presence on the Riviera ensures maximum responsiveness.
