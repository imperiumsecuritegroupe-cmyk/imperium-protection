Local presence

Exceptional security on La Croisette

Cannes is world-renowned for its Film Festival, but also for MIPIM, MIPCOM, the Advertising Festival and numerous prestigious international events. These gatherings attract celebrities, Hollywood studio executives, billionaires and senior politicians.

La Croisette concentrates an extraordinary density of high-value, high-exposure profiles. Media pressure is at its peak, paparazzi are omnipresent and attempts to intrude into private spaces are frequent. Our expertise in protection during the Cannes Film Festival and major Cannois events is well established.

Our agents know the Cannes geography perfectly: the Croisette palaces, the Californie hillside villas, yachts in the Rade de Cannes and luxury boutiques. We adapt every arrangement to the profile and agenda of our client.
