Local presence

The world capital of yachting, secured

Antibes is world-renowned for Port Vauban, Europe's largest marina, which hosts some of the world's largest superyachts. Cap d'Antibes meanwhile concentrates some of the most exclusive properties on the French Riviera, with villas selling at record prices.

Our permanent presence in Antibes and on the Cap allows us to respond rapidly to all protection needs: Cap properties, superyachts at Port Vauban, events at the Yacht Show or in prestige hotels.

We cover the entire Antibes-Juan-les-Pins area, coordinating with our Nice and Cannes teams for uninterrupted security coverage along the entire Riviera.
