Local presence

The village of artists and gourmets, protected

Mougins is a hilltop village in the Alpes-Maritimes, world-renowned for its exceptional gastronomy and long artistic tradition. Pablo Picasso spent his final years here. Today, this much-sought-after village attracts celebrities, artists and wealthy personalities visiting the French Riviera.

Located just 8 kilometres from Cannes and 20 from Nice, Mougins is ideally positioned for our teams covering the Cannes-Grasse area. We regularly operate here for the protection of residents and prestigious visitors.

Mougins benefits from a strategic position between Cannes and Grasse, within a very high-quality residential environment. The surrounding estates and properties are among the finest and most valuable in the Alpes-Maritimes.
