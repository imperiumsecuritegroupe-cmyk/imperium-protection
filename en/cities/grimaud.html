Local presence

The Provençal Venice, secured

Grimaud and Port-Grimaud form an exceptionally prestigious residential ensemble in the Gulf of Saint-Tropez. Port-Grimaud, nicknamed the Provençal Venice, is a private lakeside village where every home has its own mooring, accommodating prestigious boats and yachts.

The clientele of Grimaud and Port-Grimaud is among the wealthiest on the Var coast: owners of villas and village houses with private pontoons, and residents aboard large yachts. Our teams master this unique environment that combines maritime and land-based security.

The commune of Grimaud extends from the Var hinterland to the seafront of Port-Grimaud. This particular geography requires security management that integrates road and maritime access, surveillance of private pontoons and protection within Port-Grimaud's closed residential environment.
